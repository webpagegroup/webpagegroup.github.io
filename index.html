Project Brief

This project is established based on the investigation in Airbnb, Housing price dataset, and relevant literatures, attempt to realise better management on Airbnb development to decrease the instability and promote equilibrium of housing market in New York City. The quantitative analysis demonstrates how different Airbnb housing types and different requirement for minimum Airbnb stayed nights could influence the housing type. As well as the geospatial analysis constructed in city scale which discover the spreading of Airbnb development and housing distribution changes. And both analyses contribute to better governance of local administrator with accordingly responses effectively balancing the development of home-sharing economy in New York City.
